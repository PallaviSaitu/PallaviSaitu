Living life as it comes and letting go
as and when it needs to go.

Welcome. This is my corner of the world — a place where I gather the things I love and the ways I see. I paint, shape clay, photograph what moves me, and am slowly writing my way into a book. I believe in the beauty of impermanence, the wisdom in stillness, and the quiet courage it takes to share yourself fully.

This page is a living thing. Not everything will always be visible — and that's entirely by design. What you find here is what I've chosen to open, for now.
